"So you aren't here to harm Vera?" Sachi asked Ike cautiously, who was carrying the scrolls and globes of other planets.
"Of course not. I think she would make a great assistant." After putting down everything, he clapped his hands. "You like her?"
"Me? No, not at all, I don't like her at all!" Sachi looked away, worried that the old sage might discern his true feelings.
He then touched a box full of photo frames. He grabbed a few at random, and each picture depicted a different beauty.
"Oh, this... They all said that they gave me their one and only true love, but did they? In reality, I was just one of their many lovers."
